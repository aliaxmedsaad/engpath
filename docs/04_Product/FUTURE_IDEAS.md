# Future Ideas

Unvalidated ideas for future versions. Not commitments. Add, remove, and reprioritise freely.

---

## AI Career Coach

An AI assistant that can discuss the user's portfolio across all their projects, not just analyse individual projects in isolation.

- "Which ICE attributes am I strongest in?"
- "What kinds of projects should I pursue to fill my gaps?"
- "Am I ready to submit for Chartership?"

---

## Chat Across All Projects

Conversational interface that has access to all of the user's projects and evidence simultaneously. Allows open-ended questions about the portfolio as a whole.

---

## ICE Chartership Readiness Score

A score or indicator showing how close the user is to having sufficient evidence for Chartership submission. Based on breadth and depth of evidence across ICE attributes.

---

## Experience Gap Analysis

Specific identification of which ICE attributes are under-evidenced, along with suggestions for types of work or projects that would address the gap.

---

## ICE Export

Export a formatted evidence summary in a structure suitable for submitting to ICE directly. Would need to match ICE's expected format.

---

## CV Generation

Automatically generate a CV or professional summary based on the user's project portfolio and approved evidence.

---

## Mentor Dashboard

Allow a mentor or reviewer to:
- View a graduate's portfolio
- Leave comments on evidence items
- Mark evidence as sufficient or flag for improvement
- Track overall readiness across multiple graduates

---

## Company Accounts

Allow an employer to:
- Manage multiple graduate engineers under one account
- See portfolio progress across the cohort
- Assign mentors to graduates

---

## Support for Other Competency Frameworks

The current product is built around ICE attributes, but similar frameworks exist for other institutions (e.g. ICStructE, CIBSE, CIOB). Future versions should support multiple frameworks, with users choosing which one applies to them.

See [ICE_MAPPING.md](ICE_MAPPING.md) for current approach and design intent.
